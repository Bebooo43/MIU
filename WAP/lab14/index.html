Welcome to Lab 14


You will make use of AJAX and JSON to implement the application as a decoupled client-server, service-oriented architecture. Meaning, your webapp client will be separate from the backend RESTful service (which is already implemented and provided for your use). The following RESTful service endpoint urls are available to your LibraryBooks management webapp: https://elibraryrestapi.herokuapp.com/elibrary/api/book/list : Presents JSON formatted data containing list of books in and array. https://elibraryrestapi.herokuapp.com/elibrary/api/book/add : Adds a new book to the collection. Take screenshots of your webapp (see examples attached) and include in your submission to Sakai or github repo.
